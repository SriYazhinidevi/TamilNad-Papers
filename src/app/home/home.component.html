Services

Wholesaling or distributing is the sale of goods or merchandise to retailers; to industrial, commercial, institutional or other professional business users; or to other wholesalers (wholesale businesses) and related subordinated services.[1] In general, it is the sale of goods in bulk to anyone other than a standard consumer. Wholesaling is the selling of merchandise to anyone either a person or an organization other than the end consumer of that merchandise.

The company does wholesale business in medium scale and the products are imported from china and from several mills of delhi, Mumbai and Kolkata. They are the Second-hand sellers where the paper products of different types are bought directly from the Mills and exported in and out of Tamilnadu. The binding Sheets and CC fabric are taken up by the offset printers for notebook binding . The creamy sellers are GUMSHEETS where those are used for making labels for notebooks. VARNISH and CRAFT sheets are used for craft and décor purpose . BUTTERPAPER for food wrapping and cake mounting.
